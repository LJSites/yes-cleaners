// The Owners

Najam & Julia.

Regulars come for the cleaning. They stay for Najam and Julia. Najam is the in-house tailor — courteous, honest about whether a job is worth doing, and good for actual advice (like how long your hem should be).

Julia runs the front. Drop-offs are quick. Pick-ups are easier. Yes Cleaners is the kind of small, owner-run shop you keep returning to without ever quite knowing why — until you try anywhere else.

There's a fitting room on site. There's parking. There are seven days of hours.
